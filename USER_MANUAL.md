## What is OneHelp?

OneHelp is a website that helps:
- **Volunteers** find good causes to help
- **Organizations** find volunteers for their events
- **Everyone** work together to make a difference

Think of it like a matching service - volunteers find events they care about, and organizations find people to help them.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Go to the website** and click "Register"
2. **Choose your account type:**
   - **Volunteer** - If you want to help at events
   - **Organization** - If you need volunteers for your events
3. **Fill in your information:**
   - Your name
   - Email address
   - Password (make it strong!)
4. **Click "Sign Up"**

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

That's it! You're in.

## For Volunteers

### Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see your dashboard. This is your home base. Here you can:
- See upcoming events you've signed up for
- Check your volunteer hours
- View new event opportunities
- Read messages from organizations

### Finding Events to Help With

**Method 1: Browse All Events**
1. Click "Events" in the menu
2. Scroll through the list
3. Click any event to see more details

**Method 2: Filter by Your Skills**
1. First, add your skills to your profile
2. Events matching your skills will show up first

### Signing Up for an Event

1. Find an event you like
2. Click on it to see full details
3. Read the description, date, and what they need
4. Click "Register" or "Sign Up"
5. You'll get a confirmation

### Managing Your Profile

1. Click your name in the top corner
2. Click "Profile"
3. Here you can:
   - Update your photo
   - Add your skills (like "Teaching," "Cooking," "Computer Skills")
   - Write about yourself
   - Update your contact information

### Tracking Your Hours

After attending an event:
1. Go to "My Events"
2. Find the event you attended
3. The organization will mark your attendance
4. Your hours will be added to your total

### Giving Feedback

After an event:
1. Go to "My Events"
2. Click on the event you attended
3. Click "Leave Feedback"
4. Share your experience
5. This helps organizations improve!

### Messaging Organizations

1. Click "Messages" in your dashboard
2. Click on an organization's name
3. Type your message
4. Click "Send"

Use this to:
- Ask questions about an event
- Let them know you can't make it
- Share ideas

### Getting Your Certificate

After completing volunteer hours:
1. Go to "My Events"
2. Find a completed event
3. Click "Download Certificate"
4. Print or save it for your records

## For Organizations

### Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Number of upcoming events
- Total volunteers registered
- Recent applications
- Messages from volunteers

### Creating an Event

1. Click "Create Event" in your dashboard
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Event Name** - Keep it clear and simple
   - **Description** - What volunteers will do
   - **Date and Time** - When it happens
   - **Location** - Where it happens
   - **How Many Volunteers** - Number you need
   - **Skills Needed** - What skills would help (optional)

3. **Use AI to Help!** (New Feature)
   - Click "Generate with AI" button
   - Let the computer write a great description for you
   - Edit it if you want to change anything

4. Click "Create Event"

### Managing Volunteer Applications

1. Go to "Applications" in your dashboard
2. You'll see a list of volunteers who want to help
3. For each volunteer:
   - Click "View Profile" to learn about them
   - Click "Accept" to approve them
   - Click "Decline" if you can't use them
4. Accepted volunteers get a notification

### Recording Attendance

After your event:
1. Go to "My Events"
2. Click on the completed event
3. Click "Mark Attendance"
4. Check the box next to volunteers who showed up
5. Click "Save"

This is important! It helps volunteers track their hours.

### Viewing Analytics

1. Click "Analytics" in your dashboard
2. See helpful information:
   - How many people attended your events
   - Which events were most popular
   - Volunteer hour totals
   - Growth over time

### Messaging Volunteers

1. Click "Messages"
2. Click on a volunteer's name
3. Type your message
4. Click "Send"

Use messages to:
- Confirm attendance
- Send event details
- Thank volunteers
- Answer questions

### Getting Verified

Organizations need to be verified by admins:
1. Upload your organization documents
2. Wait for admin approval
3. Once verified, you can create events

Why? This keeps the platform safe and trustworthy.

### Creating Reports

1. Go to "Reports" in your menu
2. Choose what kind of report:
   - **Event Participation** - Who came to your event
   - **Organization Summary** - Your overall stats
3. Click "Generate Report"
4. Download as PDF or Excel

Use these for:
- Grant applications
- Board meetings
- Annual reports

## For Administrators

### Your Dashboard

As an admin, you see:
- Total users in the system
- Events waiting for approval
- Organizations needing verification
- System activity

### Verifying Organizations

1. Go to "Verifications"
2. See list of organizations waiting
3. Click on an organization
4. Review their documents
5. Choose:
   - **Approve** - They can now create events
   - **Reject** - Send them a message why

### Managing Users

1. Go to "Users"
2. See all volunteers and organizations
3. For each user you can:
   - View their full profile
   - Deactivate their account (if needed)
   - Delete their account (if necessary)

**Important:** Only do this if someone violates rules.

### Monitoring Events

1. Go to "Events"
2. See all events in the system
3. You can:
   - View event details
   - Cancel inappropriate events
   - Help resolve issues

### Viewing System Reports

1. Go to "Reports"
2. Click "System Summary"
3. See:
   - Total volunteers
   - Total organizations
   - Total hours volunteered
   - Platform growth
4. Download report for records

### Managing Skills List

1. Go to "Skills"
2. Add new skills to the system
3. Edit existing skills
4. Remove outdated skills

This helps volunteers and organizations match better.

## Common Questions

### "I Forgot My Password"

1. Click "Login"
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

### "How Do I Cancel My Event Registration?"

1. Go to "My Events"
2. Find the event
3. Click "Cancel Registration"
4. Confirm you want to cancel

**Note:** Please tell the organization if you cancel close to the event date.

### "I'm Not Getting Notifications"

1. Check your email spam/junk folder
2. Go to Profile â†’ Settings
3. Make sure notifications are turned on
4. Verify your email address is correct

### "The Event Location Changed"

**For Organizations:**
1. Go to "My Events"
2. Click "Edit" on the event
3. Update the location
4. Volunteers get notified automatically

**For Volunteers:**
Check your email or messages for updates from the organization.

### "How Do I Delete My Account?"

1. Go to "Account Settings"
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click "Deactivate Account"
4. Confirm your choice

**Note:** You can reactivate anytime by logging back in within 30 days.

### "Can I Volunteer for Multiple Events on the Same Day?"

Yes! As long as the times don't overlap. Make sure you can actually attend both.

### "What If I Have a Bad Experience?"

1. First, try talking to the organization directly
2. If that doesn't work, contact an administrator
3. Use the feedback system to report issues
4. Admins review all feedback

### "How Do I Export My Volunteer Hours?"

1. Go to "My Profile"
2. Click "Activity Report"
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Download Report"
5. Save the PDF

Use this for school requirements, job applications, or personal records!

### "The AI Description Generator Isn't Working"

This feature needs special setup. If you see an error:
1. The button won't work without an AI key
2. You can still write descriptions manually
3. Contact the system administrator

## Need Help?

### Getting Support

**Contact Methods:**
- Use the "Messages" system to contact admins
- Email support (check your dashboard for email)
- Report issues through the feedback system

**Before Asking for Help:**
1. Check this manual first
2. Look at the FAQ section
3. Try logging out and back in

**When Asking for Help:**
- Describe what you were trying to do
- Explain what happened instead
- Include any error messages you see
- Mention what device/browser you're using

### Reporting Problems

If something is broken:
1. Take a screenshot if possible
2. Write down what you were doing
3. Note any error messages
4. Contact an administrator
5. Include all the above information

### Giving Feedback

We want to improve! Tell us:
- What you like
- What's confusing
- What features you wish we had
- Any bugs you find

Use the feedback form in your dashboard.

## Glossary

**Simple terms explained:**

- **Dashboard** - Your main page when you log in
- **Event** - A volunteering opportunity
- **Registration** - Signing up for an event
